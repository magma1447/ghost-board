# Simon Says

Each round Simon calls **three numbers**. Hit them in any order — one point per hit. Most points after the final round wins.

## How to play

- Every round, three random numbers light up; both players aim at the same three.
- Hitting a called number scores a point — take them in any order.
- Once both players have thrown, the next round's numbers are drawn.

## Options

- **Hit mode** — any ring counts, or restrict to doubles / triples.
- **Scoring** — flat (1 point per hit) or staggered (1, 2, 3 for the first, second, third hit of a turn).
- **Rounds** — number of rounds to play, or no limit.
- **On a tie** — end level scores as a draw, or play sudden-death rounds until someone leads.
