# Around the Clock

Work your way around the board — 1, 2, 3 … up to 20 (and optionally the bull) **in order**. First to clear them all wins.

## How to play

- Everyone starts aiming at **1**. Hit your current target to advance to the next number.
- Only the current target counts — any other number is a miss.
- Multiple hits in a single turn all count (e.g. on 5, hitting 5, then 6, then 7 advances you to 8).

## Options

- **Bull finish** — off (end at 20), or require finishing on the single or double bull.
- **Hit mode** — any ring counts, or restrict to doubles only / triples only (harder).
- **Multi-step** — a double advances two numbers and a triple three.
- **Max rounds** — draw if no one finishes within this many rounds.
