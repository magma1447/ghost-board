# Count Up

Add up your score over a fixed number of rounds. Every dart counts — the highest total wins. No busts, no checkouts: just score as much as you can. It's the inverse of X01, and a great warm-up.

## How to play

- Each turn you throw three darts; every dart adds its value to your running total.
- After the set number of rounds, the highest total wins.
- The bullseye (50) and treble 20 (60) are worth the most — go for points.

## Options

- **Rounds** — how many rounds are played (8 is the standard game).
- **Bull scoring** — outer / inner bull as 25 / 50 (standard) or 50 / 50.
- **Singles only** — count doubles and trebles as their face value (T20 = 20) for a gentler game; bull is unchanged.
- **On a tie** — end level scores as a draw, or play sudden-death rounds until someone leads.
