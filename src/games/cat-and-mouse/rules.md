# Cat and Mouse

The mouse runs clockwise around the board and the cat chases. The mouse wins by completing a full lap; the cat wins by catching up.

## How to play

- Both players advance one segment per hit, in clockwise board order.
- The **mouse** starts ahead; the **cat** starts a few steps behind (the head start).
- The mouse wins by completing the lap. The cat wins the moment it closes the gap.

## Options

- **Head start** — how many steps behind the mouse the cat begins.
- **Hit mode** — any ring counts, or restrict to doubles / triples.
- **Multi-step** — a double advances two steps, a triple three.
- **Sprint** — hit all three darts in a turn and you immediately get another three.
- **Max rounds** — an optional round limit.
- **Round limit result** — if the limit is reached and the mouse is uncaught, the mouse escapes and wins, or it's a draw.
