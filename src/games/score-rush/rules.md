# Score Rush

Race to a target score — the first player to reach it wins. Add up your darts each turn and be first past the line. No exact finish and no checkout (unlike X01): reach or pass the target and you win.

Also known as **High Score**.

## How to play

- Each turn you throw three darts; every dart adds its value to your running total.
- The first player to reach the target score wins immediately.
- Overshooting is fine — you don't have to land exactly on the target.

## Options

- **Target** — the score to race to (300 is a common target).
- **Bull scoring** — outer / inner bull as 25 / 50 (standard) or 50 / 50.
- **Singles only** — count doubles and trebles as their face value (T20 = 20) for a gentler game; bull is unchanged.
